Frequently Asked Questions

How do you determine the value of my gold?

We consider three main factors: 1) The current market price of gold (which fluctuates daily), 2) The purity of your gold (measured in karats), and 3) The weight of your items. Our goldsmiths use professional equipment for precise measurements.

What types of gold do you buy?

We buy all types of gold, including jewelry (rings, necklaces, bracelets, earrings), coins, dental gold, and scrap gold. We accept gold in various purities (10K, 14K, 18K, 21K, 22K, 24K).

Do I need an appointment?

While appointments are recommended for faster service, walk-ins are always welcome during our business hours. You can schedule an appointment through our website or by calling us.

How do I get paid?

We offer instant cash payments. For larger transactions, we can also arrange for bank transfers, checks, or store credit with a 10% bonus for future purchases.
